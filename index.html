FAQ

Questions, answered

How is this different from Mealime or FoodiePrep?

Those are excellent generalist planners. Sow's Pantry is built for one job they don't do well: helping families — especially Indian households — move off carb-heavy, white-rice-heavy eating toward protein- and fiber-forward, lower-glycemic meals, with real per-dish nutrition and a pantry-aware grocery list.

Do I need to be vegetarian?

No. Most dishes are veg or egg-based, with a few iconic non-veg options (Bengali fish, tandoori chicken) clearly marked. Filter to what you eat.

Does it really save money?

A pantry-aware list prevents duplicate buys — the biggest quiet source of grocery waste. You shop for what you're missing, not what you already have.

Which devices work?

Any modern phone, tablet or computer. Open it in a browser and "Add to Home Screen" — it installs like a native app and works offline.

Is my data private?

Your pantry and plans are yours. On Pro, they sync privately across your own devices. We don't sell your data.

Is this medical or nutrition advice?

No. Sow's Pantry offers general healthy-eating guidance, not medical advice. If you're diabetic, pregnant, or on medication, confirm targets with a clinician.
