Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

⚠️ Starter template — have it reviewed and complete the data-processor details before launch.

What we collect

Account email (for login), and the pantry, recipes, plans and photos you create. Basic usage/diagnostic data.

How we use it

To run the Service — store your data, sync it across your devices, and process subscription payments. We do not sell your personal data.

Processors

We use trusted providers to operate: Supabase (database & authentication), Vercel (hosting), Stripe (payments), and Cloudinary (photos you upload). Each processes data only to provide their service.

Your rights

You can export or delete your data anytime from the app, or by emailing us. We keep data only as long as your account is active.
